This Privacy Policy explains how the Company collects, uses, and protects personal data of individuals who interact with or use our services (the “Services”).

This Privacy Policy governs how your personal data is collected, used, and stored.

Your Personal Data may be processed by the Company, its authorized employees or agents, and, where applicable, trusted partners or subcontractors, strictly for the purpose of providing or supporting our Services.

Legal basis for processing

Your personal data may be shared with authorized third parties that process data on our behalf, such as service providers assisting with technical, administrative, or communication-related functions.
